Our Story

Opened 1976.
Still here.

Howard Motors opened at 3510 Monroe Road in De Pere, Wisconsin in 1976. The shop has been family-run since that first year, working on cars, trucks, and SUVs for De Pere drivers and selling used vehicles from the lot at the same address.

Forty-nine years later, the address has not moved and the family-run character has not changed. Most independent mechanic shops do not clear ten years. Most family-run used-car lots do not clear twenty. Monroe Road has carried Howard Motors across both marks by a wide margin, with a service bay on one side and a sales lot on the other.

How long has Howard Motors been open?

Howard Motors has operated at 3510 Monroe Road in De Pere, Wisconsin since 1976. Forty-nine years at the same address, family-run from the first year, with full-service auto repair and a used-vehicle sales lot on the same property.

Does the shop do both repair and used-vehicle sales?

Yes. The mechanic bay handles tune-ups, oil changes, transmission work, brake repair, full diagnostics, and general auto repair. The used-vehicle lot at the same address sells cars, trucks, and SUVs, with most carrying a written 6 Month / 6,000 Mile warranty.

What does the 6 Month / 6,000 Mile warranty cover?

Most cars, trucks, and SUVs sold from the lot carry a written 6 Month / 6,000 Mile warranty on the shop's own published terms. Specific exclusions, vehicle eligibility, and the claim process are walked through at the counter against the shop's published warranty language.

Is Howard Motors part of a national chain?

No. Howard Motors is independent and family-run. Not a franchise dealer and not part of a national mechanic chain. That family-run character has carried across forty-nine years on Monroe Road.

What is the best way to reach the shop?
